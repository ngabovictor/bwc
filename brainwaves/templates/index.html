About Us

About Brain Waves Company

This is a research company which has been created by Clarisse Marie Claudine Simbi in 2014.

Clarisse is Epidemiologist and Statistician; currently enrolling in PhD program in Epidemiology and Health Statistics at Ningxia Medical University in Yinchuan, China.

She is passionate about advancing the movement of global health equity. Now the company has grown and has more than 20 professional statisticians, Epidemiologists, researchers and public health practitioners working together to bring about positive change in their community by conducting influential and valuable health related researches.

OUR MISSION

Our foremost concern is promoting the awareness and access to quality health care services by conducting research.

WHO WE ARE

We are a group of Researchers, Statisticians, Epidemiologists, and Public Health practitioners who want to promote the quality health care services through conducting researches elucidating the most health challenging problems we are facing in our communities as well as finding the lasting solutions for improving the lives of Rwandan population.
